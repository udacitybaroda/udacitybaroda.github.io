What is Udacity Baroda?

Udacity Baroda Community is a platform for all Udacians in the city to meet fellow Udacians and other amazing people, explore and indulge in awesome events around the community, share relevant and useful resources.

Why join Udacity Baroda?

Learning is driven by curiosity and collaboration. Udacity Baroda aims to expand the types of learning patterns and make it more social. Learning together has always proven as a better method of exchanging ideas and knowledge and that is what we are establishing here.
